A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

Welcome to The Moksha Centre, Kerry.

We offer a wide variety of yoga, pilates and mindfulness classes, with the aim of enabling you to bring healing, balance and harmony into your life.

If you have a little experience practising yoga, have an established or advanced practice or if you are considering starting yoga please join us for the highest quality yoga classes taught by expert teachers.

We have a beautiful studio, located in a secluded, natural environment that embodies that ideas of health and well-being.

We share over a decade of experience in the field of yoga, pilates and mindfulness education and we have welcomed hundreds of students attend our classes from around the world.

All the staff at our centre are warm, friendly and supportive; all our teachers are trained to the highest standards and are certified with the Irish Yoga Association.
